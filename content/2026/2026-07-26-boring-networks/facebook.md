# Facebook — boring-networks (MZS page)

The best-run networks are boring. If your NOC is exciting, that's a symptom — not a badge of honor.

Excitement in operations is variance with good PR. Every "all hands on deck" moment means something got through: an unreviewed change, an untested failover, a workaround that finally collapsed.

A quiet network isn't lucky — it's designed. Changes reviewed and reversible, failovers tested before they're needed, intent written down. None of it makes a good war story. All of it is the job.

Exciting NOCs make great stories. Boring NOCs make great networks. Decide which one you're building.

When was your last genuinely boring month? 👇
