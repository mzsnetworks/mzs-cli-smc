# Instagram — publish-ready

You can't buy accountability. But you keep trying to, one tool at a time. 🛠️

Every incident review ends the same way: someone proposes a new platform. Six months later, the same outage.

A toolchain distributes information. It does not distribute ownership 👇
→ Every team's dashboard is green.
→ The service is still down.
→ Nobody owns the thing the customer is staring at.

That's not a tooling gap. It's an accountability gap wearing a tooling costume. ⚙️

More panes of glass = more places to point. "My dashboard was fine." Visibility up, ownership down.

A toolchain tells you what is broken.
It never tells you whose job it is to fix it.
That line you draw yourself.

Missing visibility — or is nobody actually on the hook? 👇
