# Instagram Platform Rules

Layers on top of `SHARED.md`.

## Format

- **Caption:** punchy and scannable. Short lines, frequent breaks. Lead with the hook in the first line (the rest is hidden behind "more").
- **Arrow bullets** for data: `→ AI will write 25% of configs by 2027 (was <3% in 2024)`.
- **Length:** shorter than LinkedIn. Tighten the master idea to its emotional core.

## Emoji

- Yes — purposeful, not decorative. One per key beat to mark structure (a door 🚪 for the metaphor, a scale ⚖️ for judgment, a pointer 👇 to drive to the data). Don't pepper every line.

## Carousel

- Provide slide ideas as a separate block. Pattern that works:
  1. Hook slide — one big bold number
  2. The reframe / core metaphor
  3. The tension ("the CLI isn't your job anymore")
  4. The new skill
  5. The triad / progression
  6. CTA slide — the closing question

## Hashtags

- **A block of 10–15**, kept separate from the caption (after a `---` or in the first comment).
- Mix: niche-specific (`#NetDevOps #NetworkAutomation #AIOps`) + broader reach tags (`#Networking #ITCareers #TechFuture`). Instagram discovery rewards breadth, unlike LinkedIn.
- Still technical-leaning. No spam tags unrelated to the content.

## Calibration

The reference `content/2026/2026-06-24-ai-makes-us-judges/instagram.md` is the target: emoji-accented caption, arrow-bullet data, the same triad landing, a closing question, a 15-tag block, and six carousel slide ideas.
